The Ghost in the Heart of Tarven

Available on Amazon now, check it out! It's free if you have Kindle Unlimited!

Check out this sci-fi corporate espionage novella with a Midcentury Americana vibe and more than a dash of fantasy and office romance! Includes a generous side of office banter and corporate humor!

Ludeng Corporation has done the improbable. They have developed a device, inserted just below the heart, that makes people live three times longer.

But the Heart is more miracle than scientific breakthrough. The source of its power and the mechanism by which it operates are a mystery. Not even the brightest minds in the Shilloan Empire have been able to reverse engineer the technology.

What makes the Heart of Tarven beat?

That is the question that drives Dian Corporation to send a failed scientist and disappointing son of privilege, Birik Lev, to infiltrate Ludeng in a desparate attempt to rescue their falling stock price.

His only source inside Ludeng: a fellow new employee Adiri Roni, a failed entrepreneur falling back on a corporate career, who knows more than she appears to.

Birik learns Ludeng's labs hold more than machinery and test tubes.

He finds lies, love, consequences, and an old power all but forgotten.
